Mitchell is in his fourth year of a combined Arts (Major in Film Studies) and Media (PR and Advertising) degree at UNSW. He is interested in researching the ways that film and digital media intersect with human and non-human animals, as well as the impacts new media has on aesthetics and identity. He also has a strong interest in poststructuralist and posthumanist approaches to politics and philosophy.
