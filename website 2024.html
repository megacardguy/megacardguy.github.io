Mitchell Price is a writer and researcher based on unceded Bedegal land. His interests are in data infrastructures, mediation, and the diagrammatic.

Mitchell currently works as a research assistant at the University of New South Wales for the Media Futures Hub and Data Justice Research Network.

He also produces and co-hosts a fortnightly podcast on radical politics and pop culture with journalist and writer (and wife) Soaliha Iqbal called Here's the Thing, Tho. Episodes can be found on Apple Podcasts, Spotify, or wherever podcasts are distributed.
